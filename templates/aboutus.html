About Roaring Lions

Roaring Lion Tours and Travel Ltd is a premier provider of unforgettable travel experiences, offering expertly curated tours to destinations around the globe. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for adventure, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel ensures that every journey is a once-in-a-lifetime experience. Contact: Princess Eguchi, Chief Executive Officer

Roaring Lion Tours and Travel Ltd, a leading provider of unforgettable travel experiences, is thrilled to unveil an exciting array of adventures for 2024. From exotic wildlife safaris to cultural immersion journeys, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel invites adventurers of all ages to explore the wonders of Africa with expert guides and unparalleled service.

With a commitment to delivering exceptional travel experiences, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel has curated a diverse selection of destinations and itineraries designed to satisfy every wanderlust. Whether it’s witnessing the majestic beauty of East Africa’s Kilimanjaro, discovering West Africa’s cultural diversity from food to language or embarking on a historical journey through North Africa’s ancient cities of Cairo and Timbuktu, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel promises to exceed your expectations as you experience your travel dreams.

“We are delighted to unveil our lineup of adventures for 2024,” said Princess Eguchi, CEO of Roaring Lion Tours and Travel. “At Roaring Lion, we believe that travel should be more than just a vacation-it should be an immersive, transformative experience that leaves you yearning for more. That is why we have meticulously handpicked each destination and crafted unique itineraries that allow our guests to truly connect with the world around them.

In addition to traditional sightseeing tours, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel offers specialty experiences such as photography safaris, culinary adventures, and eco-friendly travel options. With a focus on sustainability and responsible tourism practices, Roaring Lion Tours is committed to preserving the planets natural beauty for future generations to enjoy. As an organization, we are committed to empowering the communities that live in and around the places where we operate, therefore through our corporate social responsibility (CSR) programs we provide water and sanitation facilities to such communities, To ensure the safety and wellbeing of guests, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel has implemented rigorous health and safety protocols in accordance with global guidelines. From enhanced sanitation measures to flexible booking policies, Roaring Lion Tours and Travel is dedicated to providing a safe and worry-free travel experience for all their travelers.

Sustainability

No matter where you go or how you travel, you will have an effect on the environment and the people you visit, that is why we strongly believe that sustainable tourism is key to success in our business.
