Diani beach

Just south of Mombasa is the famous Diani beach, one of the most renowned on the East African Coast, with its long, soft, white sand making it a tropical paradise. There is also an offshore reef which is ideal for snorkelling and as this also keeps the waves low it is an ideal beach for paddling and swimming. Diani beach is suitable for honeymooners, families and watersports enthusiasts. From the end of June through December, humpback whales migrate along the Kenyan coast. There are also many exclusive hotels situated on the beach.
