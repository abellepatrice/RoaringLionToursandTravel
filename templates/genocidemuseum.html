Kigali Genocide Museum

No visit to Rwanda would be complete without a visit to the Kigali Genocide Memorial Centre, or ‘Gisozi’. This is where 250,000 victims from the 1994 genocide were buried. The three permanent exhibitions and burial gardens form part of a meaningful tribute to those who perished, and provide a powerful educational tool for visitors. https://www.kgm.rw/
