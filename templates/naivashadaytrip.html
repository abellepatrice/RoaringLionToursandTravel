Lake Naivasha Day Trip

Lake Naivasha Hells Gate Game Drive, Boating on Lake Naivasha, Walking Safari, Bike ride Safari
1.Early morning pick up from Nairobi hotel 07:00am and drive through the great rift valley escarpment.
2.The drive takes one hour few minutes.
3.Arrive Naivasha where we offer boat safaris on Lake Naivasha which is the highest lake in the Rift Valley at 6000ft. Fringed by papyrus and with the extinct Mount Longonot as a backdrop, this is a wonderful place to watch and photogra/ph birds. Over 400 species of birds have been seen in the Rift Valley, and Lake Naivasha offers great bird viewing opportunities. Get close to Cormorants drying their wings, Pink beacked Pelicans, Herons, colorful Kingfishers and Fish Eagles. After boat ride you will break for bike ride proceeding to Hell’s Gate National Park which is known for its interesting geology and the steam geysers that are found in some areas. The cliffs in the park provide ideal nesting and vantage points for many birds of prey. It is possible to hike down the gorge to the hot springs and one of our resident naturalists will be happy to accompany you on the walk.
4.After you will have your late lunch at Lake Naivasha Sopa lodge.
5.After lunch drive back to Nairobi.
6.Arrive in Nairobi around 6pm drop off to your respectively hotel or drop off Airport for your flight back home.
